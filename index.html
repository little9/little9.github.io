Hi, I'm Jamie.

I'm a software developer in Kansas City who works at Kicksite.

I mostly code in Ruby and JavaScript, but have used Python, Java, and PHP in professional contexts before.

I worked at Data Curation Experts before starting at Kicksite. During that time, I worked on projects for Emory University, Tufts, UC-Santa Barbara, Northwestern, Indiana University, OHSU, and PSU.

Earlier, I was the digital preservation and software development librarian at the University of Miami.

Before that, I was a GA at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign where I wrote and maintained software for the iSchool Research Services department and contributed development work to research projects.
