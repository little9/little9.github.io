I'm the digital preservation and application development librarian at the University of Miami Libraries.

My research interests include digital preservation, markup languages, and software design.

I'm currently the maintainer for the Ruby bagit gem and am using Celery and python to automate our uploads to the Academic Preservation Trust.

Some other open source projects that I have worked on include UM's APTrust Automation software which was used for testing the service before it went live, UDVD a customized Blacklight interface for browsing videos, and RAMP a tool for enhancing finding aids and publishing them on Wikipedia.

I also contribute to SubjectsPlus a tool for creating subject guides for libraries that can also be used as a library focused CMS.
